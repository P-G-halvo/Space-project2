The mission

Welcome to The Astral Mission, where the cosmos is no longer a frontier but an invitation. Our mission is to bring space exploration to the masses, allowing ordinary people to experience the extraordinary. We are committed to pioneering commercial space travel, with our flagship journey: orbiting Jupiter. Join us as we explore the wonders of the universe.

What to expect

Embarking on a mission to orbit Jupiter is an adventure like no other. The journey begins with a thrilling launch, followed by a serene cruise through the vastness of space. As we approach Jupiter, passengers will witness breathtaking views of the planet's surface and its many moons. Our expert crew will provide informative lectures and interactive experiences, ensuring that you return to Earth with a deeper understanding of our solar system.
