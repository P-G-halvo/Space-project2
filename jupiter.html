The mission

Welcome to The Astral Mission, where the cosmos is no longer a frontier but an invitation. Our mission is to bring space exploration to the masses, allowing ordinary people to experience the extraordinary. We are committed to pioneering commercial space travel, with our flagship journey: orbiting Jupiter. Join us as we explore the wonders of the universe.

The planet

Jupiter, the giant of our solar system, is a breathtaking destination that has fascinated humanity for centuries. Known for its vibrant, swirling clouds and the iconic Great Red Spot, this gas giant is a marvel of celestial beauty. Jupiter's immense magnetic field, numerous moons, and mysterious core make it an intriguing target for scientific discovery and an awe-inspiring destination for space tourists.
